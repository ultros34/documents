Privacy Policy

Tend — Effective Date: March 15, 2026

Overview

Tend is a daily affirmations and intentions app developed by Eric Klein. This privacy policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

The short version: Tend does not collect or store any personal data on external servers. Everything you add to the app stays on your device.

Data Stored on Your Device

Tend stores the following data only on your device using local storage. This data is never transmitted to any server:

  • Your daily intentions (to-do items)
  • Your selected theme preference
  • Your app preferences (onboarding status, premium status)

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob Free users only

If you have not purchased Tend Premium, the app displays banner advertisements served by Google AdMob. Google may collect the following to serve and personalize ads:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device and app usage information

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Premium users do not see ads and are not subject to AdMob data collection.

Google Play Billing

If you purchase Tend Premium, the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play. I do not receive or store your payment information. Google's handling of purchase data is governed by the Google Play Terms of Service.

What I Do Not Collect

  • I do not operate any servers or databases
  • I do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information
  • I do not track your location
  • I do not share any data with third parties beyond what is described above
  • I do not use analytics services

Children's Privacy

Tend is not directed at children under the age of 13. I do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact me so I can address it.

Data Deletion

All locally stored data (intentions, preferences) can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app. Since no data is stored on external servers, uninstalling the app removes all associated data completely.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
