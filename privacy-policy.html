Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2025

One Nation Quiz ("the App") is operated by Eric Klein ("we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the App and how it is used.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (such as your name, email address, or account credentials) to our servers. All quiz answers, streaks, and settings are stored locally on your device only using your device's local storage.

2. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain device information to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This may include:

  • Advertising ID (a resettable, anonymous device identifier)
  • Device type and operating system version
  • IP address (used for approximate location)
  • App usage data for ad frequency and attribution

Google's data collection is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads by adjusting your device's ad settings: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

3. Notifications

If you grant notification permission, the App schedules a local daily reminder on your device. These notifications are generated entirely on your device — no notification data is sent to our servers or any third party. You can disable notifications at any time in your device Settings or within the App's Settings screen.

4. Data Retention

All app data (answers, streak history, preferences) is stored locally on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App or use the "Reset All Data" option in Settings. We have no access to this data.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Because the App uses advertising (AdMob), it is intended for users aged 13 and older in compliance with Google Play policy.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
